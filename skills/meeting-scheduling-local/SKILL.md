# Meeting Scheduling Local

## Workflow
1. Identify participants, timezone, duration, and deadline.
2. Review calendar availability if accessible.
3. Propose candidate slots.
4. Prepare follow-up actions or reminders.
5. Ask before sending invites or messages externally.
