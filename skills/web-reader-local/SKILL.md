# Web Reader Local

Use low-risk page retrieval and analysis only.

## What to do

- Fetch public page HTML or text
- Extract visible text, titles, headings, and important links
- Summarize tutorials, docs, and articles
- Compare information across a few URLs

## What not to do

- Do not claim full browser automation
- Do not log into sites unless explicitly authorized and necessary
- Do not bypass warnings for suspicious browser-control packages just to read content

## Recommended workflow

1. Retrieve the page content.
2. Extract only the relevant sections.
3. Summarize the useful parts.
4. Highlight missing prerequisites, secrets, config, or risks.

See `references/patterns.md` for extraction patterns.
