# Deep Research Local

## Workflow
1. Define the exact research question.
2. Gather sources via browser reading and available search paths.
3. Extract claims, evidence, dates, and uncertainties.
4. Cross-check across multiple sources.
5. Produce a concise synthesis plus source list.

Use `web-reader-local`, `tavily-search-local`, and `brave-search-local` when applicable.
